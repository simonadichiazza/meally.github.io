INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 onion
  • 1,5 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1,5 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1,5 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 x 400g cans tomatoes
  • 1 x 400g cans black beans
  • Rice

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 20 min

In a large pot, heat the olive oil and fry the garlic and onions.
Add chilli powder and cumin and let cook for a few minutes.
Add the vinegar, sugar, tomatoes and some seasoning.
Cook for 10 minutes.
Pur in the beans and cook for another 10 minutes.
Cook the rice.

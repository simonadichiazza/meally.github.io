INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1/2 msk grated ginger
  • 125 frozen soybeans
  • ¼ dl sweet chili sauce
  • 1 msk soya
  • ¼ dl water
  • 1 halloumi
  • 1/2 msk oil
  • 2 port egg noodles

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 20 min

Shred the peppers and grate the ginger.
Mix sweet chili sauce, soya, ginger and water.
Fry the halloumi and season as desired.
Add pepper, soybeans and the sauce that you mixed in the pan.
Cook the noodles and mix everything together.

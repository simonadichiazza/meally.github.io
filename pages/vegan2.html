INGREDIENTS

  • 0,5 aubergine
  • 200 g pasta
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 0,5 yellow onion
  • 0,5 msk olive oil
  • 195 g crushed tomatoes
  • 25 ml water
  • 0,5 pot of basil

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 20 min

Peel and finely chop the garlic and onion and fry in olive oil.
Add crushed tomatoes and water and simmer for about 5 minutes.
Wipe the eggplant with kitchen paper.
Fry it in a new pan with olive oil.
Put the eggplant in the tomato sauce and bring to the boil.
Season as you desire.
Cook pasta and mix with the sauce.
Put basil on top.

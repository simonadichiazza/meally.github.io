INGREDIENTS

  • 1 chilled pie dough (230 g)
  • 5 eggs
  • 3 dl milk
  • 1 dl cream
  • 500 g frozen, thawed kale
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 0,5 pot basil
  • 150 g feta cheese

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 40 min

Cover the bottom of a pie tin with the pie dough.
Notch with a fork.
Pre-bake in the middle of the oven for 6-8 minutes.
Whisk the eggs lightly with milk and cream.
Add salt and pepper.
Let the kale drain in a strainer, squeeze out the liquid.
Spread kale, onion, garlic, basil
and crumbled feta cheese over the pre baked pie crust.
Pour over the egg punch and garnish in the oven for 35 minutes.

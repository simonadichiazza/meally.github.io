INGREDIENTS

  • 2 dl dried, red lentils
  • 3 carrots
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter/tahini
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • 1 tbsp potato flour
  • 1 tbsp sesame seeds

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 20 min

Soak the red lentils and then pour the water away.
Roughly grate the carrots.
Mix lentils, garlic clove and peanut butter/tahini finely in a blender.
Mix in carrot grate, potato flour, sesame seeds and parsley.
Salt and pepper.
Make 8 steaks and fry in oil over medium heat.
Serve with rice/mashed potatoes or sallad.

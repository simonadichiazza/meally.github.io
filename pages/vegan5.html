INGREDIENTS

  • 1 tetra with mixed beans
  • 1 dl corn
  • 2 large tomatoes
  • 1 avocado
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 msk oil
  • 2 tortilla breads

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 20 min

Pour water of the beans and rinse.
Finely chop the onion.
Dice the avocado and tomato.
Mix all ingredients in a large bowl and pour over oil.
Salt and make a burrito.

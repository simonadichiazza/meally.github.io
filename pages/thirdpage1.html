INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cloves garlic
  • 100 g mushrooms
  • 0,5 zucchini
  • 0,5 dl sunflower seeds
  • 0,5 pot basil
  • 1,5 dl oat milk
  • 0,5 dl grated cheese
  • Pasta of your choice

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 30 min

Chop the garlic cloves and fry in oil.
Chop the mushrooms and let them simmer until they are soft.
Salt and pepper.
Roughly grate the zucchini and squeeze out the water.
Add the zucchini in the pan with the garlic and mushrooms.
Mix sunflower seeds and oat milk so it becomes creamy.
Pour over the “oat cream” and stir in the cheese.
Cook and add pasta. Top with basil.

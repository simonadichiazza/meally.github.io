INGREDIENTS

  • 1 can with tuna in water
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 dl creme fraiche
  • 1/2 tsk curry powder
  • Sallad
  • 1/2 apple
  • 2 flatbreads

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 10 min

Let the tuna drain well.
Finely chop the onion.
Mix the tuna with creme fraiche, curry, salt and pepper.
Spread the mixture on flatbreads.
Add salad and apple.
Roll up.

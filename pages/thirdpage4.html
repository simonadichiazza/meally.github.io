INGREDIENTS

  • 2 msk oil
  • 2 chicken breast
  • 1/2 yellow onion
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1,5 tsk curry powder
  • 1/2 apple
  • 1 dl water
  • 1,5 dl cream or coconut milk
  • 1 vegetable broth
  • Rice

DIRECTIONS

Time required: 30 min

Cut the chicken and fry it in a pan.
Finely chop the onion and add it to the pan.
Add garlic, curry powder, vegetable broth, salt and pepper.
Grate the apple and add to the pan.
Add cream/coconut milk and simmer on low heat.
Cook the rice.
